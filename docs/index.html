Introduction to Corgis

Corgis are easily recognizable because of their stumpy legs and stout bodies.

Although they are most famous for being Queen Elizabeth II's favourite dog (she owned some 30 of them over time!), corgis were originally bred to be herding dogs. Their short stature made them ideal for this job, as they would be able to avoid larger animals trying to kick them away.

Corgis also make awesome pets! They are very intelligent and trainable dogs, and generally love being around people. However, be warned, they shed. A. Lot.
